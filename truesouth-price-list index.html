How pricing works

What goes into the price

My pricing reflects six things: my time, my gear, my skill, the creative direction, the deliverables, and the value of the result to you. Every project is different, which is why each quote is built around what you actually need rather than a fixed menu. Here's what's behind those numbers.

01

Time

Pre-production, shoot days, and editing. For every hour on camera, expect three to five hours in the edit suite.

02

Gear

Professional camera bodies, cinema lenses, drone, audio kit, tripods, and storage media.

03

Skill

Skills in composition, light, pacing, colour, and sound. You're paying for the eye, not just the equipment.

04

Creative direction

Mood boards, shot lists, location planning, narrative shaping. The film is made before we press record.

05

Deliverables

Number of films, formats, social cut-downs, revisions, usage rights, and how quickly you need it all.

06

Value

A film that earns trust and converts viewers is worth more than one that just fills a feed. Pricing reflects outcomes.
